Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

CavemanDetector does not require account registration. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords. We use Cloudflare Analytics to collect anonymized traffic data (page views, country, device type). No cookies are used for tracking.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Places API — to retrieve business data from public listings
  • OpenAI API — to generate sales message suggestions
  • Cloudflare — for DNS, CDN, and anonymized analytics
  • DigitalOcean — for hosting

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any user data on our servers. Search queries and AI-generated messages are processed in real time and are not saved.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage solely to enforce hourly usage limits (search and chat rate limiting). This data never leaves your device.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: cavemandetector@gamil.com
